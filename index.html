About Us

Your Partner in Durable Medical Equipment

Midnight Arctic Solutions LLC was founded with a clear mission: to make quality durable medical equipment accessible, affordable, and reliably delivered to patients and healthcare providers alike.

As a licensed and insured DME distributor, we work with healthcare facilities, nursing homes, physical therapy centers, and individual patients to supply the equipment they need — when they need it. Our team brings years of industry experience in medical equipment distribution, compliance, and customer service.

We believe that getting the right equipment should be a smooth, transparent process — not a complicated ordeal. That's why our quote-first model removes the friction and lets you focus on what matters: patient care.

Quality Focused — We only distribute equipment from reputable manufacturers that meets HCPCS standards.
Compliance Driven — We maintain all required state and federal licenses for DME distribution.
People First — Our team is here to support you through every step of the process.
